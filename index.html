Travel

Whenever the chance presents itself, I try and take the opportunity to get out and do some sight-seeing

San Francisco

Muir Woods. The size of the redwoods is humbling and the canopy is so far above that each tree casts a monstrous shadow on everything around it.

Agra

Home of the Taj Mahal, Red Fort, and many other ancient sites. Agra was a wonderful place to travel around all day - whether on foot or by auto-rickshaw.

Colorado

Rocky Mountain National Park. One of the more gorgeous sites I've seen. Hiking around in the high altitude proved to be quite the challenge while climbing the steep hills.

New Delhi

Delhi was host to many ancient temples, ruins, and other fascinating sites. I spent a day just visitng ancient Hindu and Muslim sites that were built thousands of years ago.

Seattle

One of my favorite cities to visit. I've been several times and have spent many days just walking the streets looking at the architecture, parks, and other sites offered by the wonderful city.

Amsterdam

I had the good fortunate of being able to spend a day just walking the streets of Amsterdam. I visited the canal district, among many other locations.
